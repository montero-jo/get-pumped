Glossary

Reps

The number of times you do an exercise before you take a break.

e.g.: doing 12 reps of a squat press means you squat a total of 12 times before resting.

Set

A number of exercises that mean to fully work a muscle. Composed of reps.

e.g.: the phrase "3 sets of 12" signifies that your 12 reps of a specific exercise are repeated thrice.
