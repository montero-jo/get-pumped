Regular exercise is on the most beneficial habits any one person can have. It’s a great way to stay healthy, reduce anxiety, improve brain function, boost your immune system, etc.

As someone who’s been going to the gym regularly for the last 3 years, I couldn’t recommend it enough. I've come to understand the gym as an environment to help me grow both physically and mentally.

Unfortunately, a large majority don't feel that way. Many people feel as if the gym is a place in which they wouldn't feel safe in. A steady exercise routine can be very intimidating to approach for newcomers, and having to adapt to a new social environment is another added level of difficulty.

That’s where Get Pumped!! comes in. This website is an archive of sorts detailing everything that I learned as an amateur gym rat over the last 3 years. You’ll find tips and tricks regarding routines, exercises to follow, healthy mindsets to stick by, and other tid bits of information to help guide you!

Have fun!
